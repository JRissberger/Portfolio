Featured Project: Astro Outlaw

In this bullet hell and exploration hybrid, you play as a pirate on her quest to travel the galaxy and find the ultimate treasure. Gather important resources on the planets you land on, before returning to space to travel to your next destination. But watch out--your rivals will stop at nothing to destroy your ship before you make it to your next destination. You'll have to use all the resources at your disposal to fight your way through space!

  • This project was created with a team of 5 other students.
  • Developed in Unity, utilizing C#.
  • Primarily developed the player's controls, interactions, and abilities in combat.
  • Additionally developed the game's boss fight, which currently acts as the final challenge of the game.
  • Assisted with conducting and documenting playtests, miscellaneous tasks, and bug fixes as needed.
